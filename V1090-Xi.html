Beyond Fast

Immerse yourself in the cutting-edge technology that powers the ArmWare Supreme AX 6090 50GB GDDRX (10) OverClocked Edition. This is no ordinary GPU; it’s the previous innovation before GDDRX. Engineered to break limits and push performance to extremes in its time, the V 1090 Xi introduced speeds and technologies that seemed impossible. With 30Gbps GDDRiX (9) memory and a 2220MHz clock speed, it was designed to handle anything from real-time neural processing to quantum physics simulations.

Beneath its rugged exterior, the front view showcases three intricately designed fans with advanced liquid-cooled heat dissipation channels, accented by dynamic RGB lighting that pulses in harmony with your system. The RGB glow, tucked behind the fans, reacts to temperature, shifting from calm blues under low loads to fiery reds during peak performance, ensuring both stunning visuals and optimal cooling.

The quad-layer polymer fan blades provide ultra-silent operation, spinning at up to 16,000 RPM while a nano-graphene coating ensures dust-free operation, improving longevity and efficiency. Behind this cooling marvel lies the beating heart of the GPU—a custom-designed V Quantum Accelerator, built to predict and optimize frame rates in real time with its AI Frame Generation engine.

On the side, the ArmWare logo lights up in a stunning RGB infinity loop, creating a never-ending cascade of colors that you can fully customize. The V 1090 Xi boasts a PCIe 4.5 Hybrid X-slot, offering double the bandwidth of standard PCIe 4.5, enabling ultra-fast data transfer rates of up to 368 GB/s.

Additional dual 16-pin HyperVolt connectors supply unprecedented power, enabling overclocking like never before. The QuantumBoost AI power regulators adjust voltage in real-time, protecting your hardware from surges while maintaining maximum performance. This GPU doesn’t just perform—it predicts, learns, and evolves to your usage patterns.

The backplate is a work of art, featuring a titanium-plated heat shield with laser-etched circuits that double as aesthetic RGB channels. ArmWare's proprietary LumenShift technology illuminates the rear, synchronizing with the front RGB to produce a harmonious light show across your entire build. Embedded into the backplate is a graphene heatsink that optimizes heat dissipation, keeping your GPU cool even under the most intense workloads.

At the core of the backplate design are the AI-Driven Connectors, which automatically adjust to the optimal power input for any given task. Whether you're overclocking to extreme levels or running the latest AAA titles at 2k Ultra settings, this backplate provides seamless support, durability, and a futuristic visual appeal.
