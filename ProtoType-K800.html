The ProtoType K-800 is designed for gamers who demand both style and performance in a compact package. Featuring a 60% layout, this keyboard eliminates unnecessary keys while maintaining all essential functions for gaming, making it the perfect choice for minimalist setups and LAN parties. The entire keyboard is illuminated by fully customizable RGB lighting, allowing you to personalize every key and create mesmerizing lighting effects that sync with your gaming environment.

The sleek, compact form of the ProtoType K-800 is paired with Dust-Proof Red Switches, providing smooth and quiet keystrokes without compromising on speed or responsiveness. These switches ensure a long lifespan, even under heavy use, while giving you the tactile feedback needed for fast-paced gaming. With a 1ms latency, every keystroke registers instantly, giving you a competitive edge in every game. The RGB lighting radiates from every key, creating a vibrant display that is sure to impress.

The absence of software allows for easy plug-and-play functionality, so you can game without hassle, right out of the box.

The side view of the ProtoType K-800 highlights its unique Side RGB lighting, giving your desk an extra level of visual appeal. The side-mounted lights not only enhance the aesthetics but also provide a subtle glow to your gaming area, immersing you in your setup even further. The angled chassis ensures ergonomic comfort for long gaming sessions, with a sturdy yet lightweight build, making it easy to transport to tournaments or LAN events.
